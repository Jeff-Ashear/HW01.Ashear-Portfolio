Jeff Ashear

Jeff Ashear has been an active composer and performer in the Denver area since 2009, doing the same in the Galatin Basin in Montana before that. Specializing in avante guarde works for small groups of orchestral instruments with acitive and interactive electronic accompanyment, sound-sculpture, and virtual instrument design, Jeff's hopes to contribute meaningfully to new music and provide immersive, challenging, active listening experiences to the audience. Jeff also enjoys climbing rock and ice. He is now learning web developement and spending time with his family whenever possible.
